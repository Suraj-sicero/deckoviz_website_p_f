# The Enterprise Guide to Use Cases, Possibilities & Experiences
**The GAVPort by Deckoviz Space Labs**

## HOW TO USE THIS GUIDE
This guide exists for one reason: to show you what becomes possible when your walls stop being passive, and your space starts becoming intelligent, experiential, cinematic, and alive.

Most enterprise customers see Deckoviz and immediately understand three or four ways they could use it. This guide exists to show you thirty, forty, fifty. Because the businesses that get the most out of Deckoviz are not the ones who use it for one thing - they are the ones who realise, gradually, that it can do nearly everything their space has ever needed to do, and more than they ever thought to ask for.

Work through this guide the way you would work through a menu. Not everything will apply to you right now. Some use cases will feel immediately obvious. Others will surprise you. Many will make you think about your space in a way you haven't before. That is the point.

This guide is organised in three layers. First, general enterprise use cases that apply across all business types. Then dedicated sections for restaurants, retail stores, and hotels - each broken into categories, so you can navigate directly to what matters most for your context.

> **One important note before you begin:** every use case in this guide can be created, updated, and deployed from your mobile app or by voice, in seconds, with no artists, no designers, no print runs, and no technical knowledge. That is not a footnote - it changes the economics and the operational reality of everything you are about to read.

This guide will grow. As Deckoviz evolves, new use cases, new modes, and new possibilities are added regularly. Think of what you are reading as a living document - and think of your Deckoviz Portal as a living platform.

## PART ONE: GENERAL ENTERPRISE USE CASES
*Applicable across all business types and verticals.*

### 1.1 - AMBIENCE & ATMOSPHERE
- **Dynamic Time-of-Day Ambience:** Set your space to automatically shift its visual mood across the day - morning energy, afternoon calm, evening warmth - without any manual adjustment, so the atmosphere always matches the moment.
- **Seasonal Ambience Rotation:** Update your space's entire visual identity with the seasons - spring lightness, summer warmth, autumn richness, winter stillness - keeping the environment feeling current without any physical redesign.
- **Occasion-Based Atmosphere Switching:** Instantly switch between everyday mode and special occasion modes - a birthday, a private event, a product launch, a festive celebration - at a moment's notice, with no setup time.
- **Mood Curation for Different Zones:** Set different ambience profiles for different areas of your space - a calm, intimate zone here, an energetic, high-footfall zone there - using multiple Deckoviz units to architect the emotional journey through your space.
- **Music-Synced Visual Ambience:** Pair your background music with complementary visual experiences so the audio and visuals work together to create a genuinely immersive atmosphere rather than two disconnected sensory inputs.
- **Weather-Responsive Display:** On a bright summer day or a grey winter afternoon, Deckoviz can adapt its visual mood to complement or counterbalance what's happening outside - making your interior feel consistently intentional regardless of the weather.
- **Brand Palette Ambience:** Create ambient visual experiences built entirely around your brand's colour palette and aesthetic language, so the feeling of your space and the feeling of your brand become one and the same.

### 1.2 - BRAND STORYTELLING & IDENTITY
- **Founding Story Display:** Tell the story of how your business was started - the inspiration, the vision, the early days - through beautiful visual art and narrated sequences that make guests feel the humanity behind the brand.
- **Brand Values Visualisation:** Bring your brand values to life not as text on a wall but as dynamic, evolving visual expressions - abstract artworks, symbolic imagery, and visual metaphors that communicate what you stand for without stating it.
- **Heritage and Legacy Artwork:** For established businesses, create artworks that honour your history - your founding year, key milestones, significant moments - displayed with the aesthetic quality they deserve.
- **Behind-the-Scenes Storytelling:** Show guests the craft, the care, and the people behind your offering - kitchen preparation, production processes, sourcing journeys - creating transparency that builds trust and deepens connection.
- **Team Story Display:** Feature your team - their faces, their stories, their expertise - through beautifully designed visual content that humanises your brand and creates warmth in your guest interactions.
- **Philosophy and Mission Storytelling:** Translate your mission statement from a line of text into a living visual narrative that guests absorb naturally as they move through your space.
- **Supplier and Partner Stories:** For brands that source ethically, locally, or with care, use Deckoviz to celebrate those relationships - the farm, the artisan, the producer - giving your sourcing story the visibility it earns.

### 1.3 - MARKETING & CAMPAIGNS
- **In-Space Campaign Deployment:** Launch a new campaign across your physical space in seconds - the same creative going up across all your locations simultaneously, with no print runs, no delivery delays, and no installation costs.
- **Promotional Poster Creation:** Generate beautiful, on-brand promotional posters for any offer, event, or campaign directly through the Deckoviz creation engine - no agency brief, no design file, no wait.
- **Time-Limited Offer Display:** Create a sense of urgency and exclusivity around time-limited offers with visually compelling countdown-style or offer-specific displays that are easy to set and easy to remove.
- **Event Promotion:** Promote upcoming events - a tasting evening, a launch night, a seasonal special - through dynamic in-space visuals that build anticipation in the days and weeks before.
- **New Product or Service Launch:** Give a new launch the visual impact it deserves - a full in-space takeover that makes the introduction feel momentous rather than routine.
- **Cross-Sell and Upsell Visuals:** Prompt customers towards higher-margin products, add-ons, or complementary offerings through beautifully placed visual suggestions that feel like discovery rather than sales pressure.
- **Social Media Content Creation:** Create marketing videos and visual content in-space that can be directly repurposed for your Instagram, LinkedIn, or other social channels - turning your physical space into a content production environment.
- **Partnership and Collaboration Display:** Feature brand collaborations, partnerships, or limited editions through dedicated visual moments that give your partners visibility while adding interest and freshness to your space.

### 1.4 - GUEST & CUSTOMER PERSONALISATION
- **Personalised Welcome Displays:** Greet specific guests, clients, or groups with a custom welcome artwork - "Welcome, Johnson Family" or "Welcome, Team Deloitte" - creating a moment of genuine surprise and delight.
- **Celebration Recognition:** Identify guests celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or milestone and display a personalised artwork to mark the occasion - making people feel seen and remembered in a way that no other technology can replicate.
- **VIP and Returning Guest Recognition:** For regular customers or high-value guests, create subtle and elegant personalised moments that communicate "we know you" without intrusiveness - building loyalty through feeling rather than programme mechanics.
- **Customer Memento Artwork:** Create a personalised artwork for a customer to mark their visit, purchase, or experience - something they can screenshot and share, creating an organic social moment tied to your brand.
- **Group and Event Personalisation:** For private bookings, corporate events, or group visits, create a dedicated visual experience that makes the group feel the space was designed specifically for them.

### 1.5 - OPERATIONAL & INFORMATIONAL DISPLAY
- **Real-Time Information Boards:** Display operational information - wait times, daily schedules, event timelines, floor maps - in a visually refined way that communicates clearly without looking like a corporate notice board.
- **Wayfinding and Space Navigation:** In larger spaces, use Deckoviz to help guests orient themselves - directional information, zone identification, space maps - presented beautifully rather than on generic printed signage.
- **Opening Hours and Policy Communication:** Display practical information - hours, policies, contact details - as part of a visually coherent display rather than a photocopied sheet in a plastic frame.
- **Queue Management Displays:** Make waiting feel intentional rather than frustrating by filling the wait with engaging, beautiful visuals that entertain, inform, or educate your guest while they queue.
- **Sustainability and Impact Reporting:** For businesses with genuine sustainability commitments, display your environmental impact in a visually compelling format - carbon saved, waste reduced, sourcing choices made - making your values tangible.
- **Staff Recognition Display:** Celebrate team members - employee of the month, milestone anniversaries, new joiners - through beautifully designed visual moments that create culture and make staff feel valued.
- **Health, Safety, and Compliance Communication:** Display required compliance messaging in a visually considered way that maintains the premium feel of your space rather than undoing it with generic posters.

### 1.6 - MULTI-LOCATION & SCALE
- **Centralised Content Management:** For businesses with multiple locations, manage all your Deckoviz displays from a single dashboard - updating content, deploying campaigns, and maintaining brand consistency across every site simultaneously.
- **Local Flexibility Within Brand Consistency:** While managing content centrally, allow individual locations to adapt certain elements - a local event, a neighbourhood-specific offering, a store-specific promotion - without breaking brand cohesion.
- **A/B Testing Visual Content:** Test different promotional visuals, menu presentations, or campaign approaches across locations and measure which performs best before rolling out universally - data-driven content decisions that were never previously possible in physical retail.
- **Franchisee Content Control:** For franchise models, maintain brand standards centrally while giving franchisees appropriate flexibility to respond to their local audience and occasion.
- **Scheduled Content Calendar:** Plan your visual content weeks or months in advance - seasonal campaigns, promotional periods, events - and have everything deploy automatically on the right day without manual intervention.

## PART TWO: RESTAURANTS

### INTRODUCTION TO RESTAURANTS
The restaurant that gets remembered is rarely the one with the best food alone. It is the one where the experience felt complete - where the atmosphere, the storytelling, the visual environment, and the food worked together to create something that guests talk about when they leave and think about when they decide where to go next.

Deckoviz gives restaurants a tool that does more work per square foot than anything else in the space. It sets the ambience. It tells the story. It sells the food before it arrives. It handles your menus, your promotions, your celebrations, and your social media content - all from one device, updated in seconds, without a designer or a print shop.

The sections below cover every major use case for a restaurant context, from the guest experience to the kitchen, from daily operations to brand building. Use the sub-sections that are most relevant to your style of restaurant - and know that the ones that seem less relevant today may become exactly what you need as your business grows.

### 2.1 - GUEST EXPERIENCE
- **Time-of-Day Atmosphere Shift:** Breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner all feel fundamentally different in a great restaurant - and with Deckoviz, they do, automatically. The visual mood shifts with the service, so the ambience is always right without any staff effort.
- **Arrival Impression:** The moment a guest walks in, the visual environment communicates the quality, intention, and character of your restaurant before they have spoken to anyone or seen a menu. Deckoviz makes that first impression deliberate and beautiful every single time.
- **Table Wait Experience:** The period between arriving and being seated - or between ordering and receiving food - is one of the most vulnerable moments in the guest experience. Engaging, beautiful visuals that tell a story or build appetite make that wait feel like part of the experience rather than a cost of it.
- **Private Dining Personalisation:** For private dining rooms, switch instantly to a fully personalised visual environment tailored to the group - their occasion, their preferences, their name on the wall - creating a genuinely bespoke experience at no additional operational cost.
- **Birthday and Anniversary Celebrations:** Display a beautiful personalised artwork marking the occasion for guests celebrating a significant moment - something they will photograph, share, and remember. This one use case alone consistently generates organic social content for the restaurants that deploy it.
- **Mood-Based Dining Environment:** For restaurants with a distinct identity - romantic, energetic, contemplative, social - use Deckoviz to express and reinforce that identity through visuals that match the brand's emotional character, making the dining experience feel cohesive from arrival to departure.
- **Entertainment During Off-Peak Hours:** During quieter periods, engaging visual experiences maintain the quality of the environment for the guests who are there and create a reason for passers-by to step in.
- **Queue Entertainment and Engagement:** For restaurants with walk-in queues or wait areas, Deckoviz turns the queue into an extension of the experience - showing content about your food, your story, or your craft so that by the time guests sit down, they are already engaged and primed.

### 2.2 - FOOD & MENU STORYTELLING
- **Dish Photography Display:** Upload a photo of any dish and transform it into stunning, styled product photography - the kind that builds appetite before the plate arrives and makes every item look as considered as it tastes.
- **Dish Video and Animation:** Go beyond static photography with short, cinematic dish videos or animated sequences - the steam rising from a bowl, the pour of a sauce, the texture of a freshly baked crust - visual storytelling that makes food irresistible.
- **The Story Behind the Dish:** Every great dish has an origin - a region, a memory, an inspiration, an ingredient sourced with care. Display that story alongside the dish in beautifully designed visual content that turns eating into understanding.
- **Ingredient Origin Storytelling:** For restaurants that source with intention - local farms, sustainable fisheries, small producers - use Deckoviz to make that sourcing visible and celebrated, connecting guests to the provenance of what they are eating.
- **Chef Profile and Philosophy:** Introduce your head chef, their background, their philosophy, and their signature approach through beautifully crafted visual content that builds a personal connection between the guest and the person who cooked for them.
- **Seasonal Menu Launch:** When a new seasonal menu launches, use Deckoviz to give it a visual debut - a dedicated display that communicates the new direction, the new dishes, and the seasonal inspiration with the excitement and production quality it deserves.
- **Daily Special Spotlight:** Highlight today's special with visual emphasis - a dish image, a brief description, a price point - displayed beautifully and updated instantly when the special changes. No printed inserts. No handwritten blackboards.
- **Dish Pairing Suggestions:** Suggest food and drink pairings through visual displays that feel like recommendations from an expert rather than an upsell - naturally increasing guest spend while adding genuine value to their experience.
- **Nutritional and Dietary Information:** For restaurants where guests care about nutritional content, allergens, or dietary categories, display this information in a visually refined way that maintains the quality feel of the space while serving a genuine guest need.
- **Tasting Menu Narrative:** For tasting menu restaurants, use Deckoviz to narrate the progression - introducing each course, its inspiration, its ingredients, and its chef before it arrives - turning the meal into a story told in real time.

### 2.3 - MENUS & OPERATIONAL DISPLAYS
- **Dynamic Digital Menu Board:** Replace static printed menus or outdated digital displays with a beautifully designed, AI-generated menu board that reflects your brand aesthetic - updated in seconds via voice or app when items change, specials sell out, or prices are adjusted.
- **Instant Menu Update by Voice:** "Vizzy, remove the sea bass from the menu" or "Vizzy, add today's special" - menu updates happen in seconds without anyone opening a laptop or calling a designer.
- **Happy Hour and Time-Limited Display:** Happy hour begins and the display shifts automatically - promoting your offers, your combinations, and your deals in a visually compelling format that makes the promotion feel like an event rather than a discount.
- **Section-Specific Menu Display:** For larger restaurants with separate dining areas or bar menus, display different menu content in different zones - the bar showing cocktail-focused content while the dining room shows the full food menu.
- **Event-Specific Menu Display:** For set menus, prix fixe evenings, or special dining events, create a dedicated visual menu that matches the occasion and communicates the exclusivity and deliberateness of the experience.
- **Countdown to Last Orders:** Gently communicate last food or drink orders through an elegant, on-brand visual display that maintains the mood rather than breaking it with an announcement.
- **Table Reservation and Availability Information:** For restaurants with walk-in and reservation splits, display table availability information that helps guests self-organise and reduces the administrative load on front-of-house staff.

### 2.4 - UPSELL & REVENUE
- **High-Margin Item Spotlighting:** Draw attention to your highest-margin dishes, cocktails, or desserts through dedicated visual prominence - research consistently shows that visually featured items outperform non-featured items in sales, particularly in impulse categories.
- **Dessert Visual Display:** The dessert decision is almost always made on impulse - and impulse responds to visual stimulus. Displaying your desserts beautifully while guests are finishing their main course dramatically increases dessert attachment rates.
- **Cocktail and Drinks Feature:** Showcase your cocktail menu, your wine list, or your signature serves through visual content that communicates craft, character, and desirability - making the choice to order another drink feel like a discovery rather than a transaction.
- **Upsell by Time of Day:** As service shifts - from lunch to afternoon to dinner - so do the upsell opportunities. Deckoviz adapts automatically, showing coffee and dessert content after lunch, pre-dinner cocktail content in the early evening, digestifs and nightcaps later.
- **Premium Set Menu Promotion:** If you offer a premium tasting or set menu alongside an à la carte option, use Deckoviz to make the premium experience look and feel worth the upgrade - visual storytelling that justifies the price point before the question is even asked.
- **Merchandise and Retail Upsell:** For restaurants with branded merchandise, sauces, or retail products, use Deckoviz to create a visual moment around those items that feels like a natural extension of the brand rather than a gift shop corner.

### 2.5 - BRAND BUILDING & SOCIAL
- **Restaurant History and Heritage Display:** For established restaurants with a story to tell - a founding year, a neighbourhood history, a family legacy - display that heritage through beautiful artwork that deepens the guest's sense of connection to the place.
- **Award and Recognition Display:** Showcase your accolades - Michelin recognition, press features, industry awards - in a visually elegant format that communicates credibility without feeling like a trophy cabinet.
- **Guest Review and Testimonial Display:** Surface your most powerful guest reviews through beautifully designed quote visuals that serve as social proof for new visitors and reinforce quality for returning ones.
- **In-Restaurant Social Media Content Creation:** Create short, visually stunning marketing videos inside your restaurant - dish reveals, ambience sequences, chef moments - that can be posted directly to your Instagram or TikTok, turning your physical space into a content production studio.
- **Instagrammable Moment Creation:** Design a visual environment that guests naturally want to photograph - not through a neon sign asking to be shared, but through genuinely beautiful, original visual experiences that feel worth capturing.
- **Community and Neighbourhood Storytelling:** For restaurants with a strong sense of place, use Deckoviz to celebrate the neighbourhood, the community, and the local culture that the restaurant is part of - creating a sense of belonging that transcends the food.
- **Press and Media Feature Display:** Feature your most significant press coverage through beautifully designed quote and image displays that communicate external validation in a way that feels confident, not desperate.

### 2.6 - EVENTS & SPECIAL OCCASIONS
- **Live Event Visual Environment:** For music nights, chef dinners, wine tastings, or cultural events, create a dedicated visual environment that marks the occasion as distinct from a regular service - giving the event its own aesthetic identity.
- **Festive and Holiday Theming:** Deploy festival and holiday-themed visual content - Diwali, Eid, Christmas, Lunar New Year - that celebrates the occasion and connects with guests who are marking those moments, without requiring any physical decoration changes.
- **Wedding and Private Celebration Setup:** For restaurants that host private celebrations, create a fully bespoke visual environment for each booking - the couple's names, their colour palette, their aesthetic - making the venue feel as personal as if it were designed for them.
- **Corporate Event Branding:** For corporate dinners or private bookings, display the client's logo, brand colours, and event details in an elegant visual format that makes the evening feel curated and professionally executed.
- **Charity and Fundraising Event Support:** For charity evenings or fundraising dinners, use Deckoviz to display the cause, the impact, and the stories behind the charity in a way that builds emotional connection and increases generosity.
- **Cooking Class and Experience Visual Guide:** For restaurants that offer cooking experiences or classes, use Deckoviz to display step-by-step visual guides, ingredient spotlights, and technique demonstrations that enhance the learning experience.

## PART THREE: RETAIL STORES

### INTRODUCTION TO RETAIL
The store is under pressure. Online has won on convenience, on price comparison, on breadth of selection. The only thing a physical store can offer that online never can is the experience of being somewhere - the feeling of a space, the serendipity of discovery, the sensory engagement of actually being in the presence of what you are considering buying.

That experience, if it is designed well, is worth enormously more than convenience. People travel for it. They pay a premium for it. They tell other people about it. They come back for it.

Deckoviz gives retail stores the tools to design that experience properly - to make products feel alive, spaces feel intentional, and the act of browsing feel like something more than a task to complete. The use cases below span every dimension of the retail experience, from the window display to the fitting room, from the opening morning to the seasonal campaign.

### 3.1 - PRODUCT DISPLAY & STORYTELLING
- **Product Photography Enhancement:** Transform a standard product photo into stunning, styled visual art that communicates the quality, character, and desirability of the item.
- **Product Video and Animation:** Bring products to life with short, cinematic sequences - fabrics in motion, materials in light, products in context - visual storytelling that conveys things a static image never can.
- **Product in Context Visualisation:** Show products being used, worn, or lived with in aspirational real-world settings - helping customers move from "I see it" to "I want it".
- **Category Feature Loops:** Cycle through your core product categories in a flowing visual loop that gives every part of your range a moment of prominence.
- **New Arrival Spotlight:** Give new products the visual debut they deserve - a dedicated moment that communicates freshness, excitement, and desirability.
- **Best-Seller and Hero Product Feature:** Identify your highest-performing products and give them consistent visual prominence - creating a gravitational pull.
- **Limited Edition and Exclusive Display:** For limited runs, exclusive collaborations, or numbered products, create a visual experience around the scarcity and exclusivity that makes the item feel unmissable.
- **Materials and Craftsmanship Storytelling:** Show the detail, the texture, the construction, and the care that goes into your products - slow storytelling that communicates quality.
- **Size and Variant Showcase:** Display the full range of colours, sizes, or variants available for a product in a visually compelling format.

### 3.2 - CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE & ENGAGEMENT
- **In-Store Discovery Experience:** Design a visual environment that makes browsing feel like a curated experience rather than a search.
- **Customer Visualisation Display:** Help customers see themselves in what you are selling - styled lifestyle imagery.
- **Social Proof and Customer Story Display:** Feature your real customers - their purchases, their stories, their testimonials - in beautifully designed visual content that provides authentic validation.
- **Interactive Discovery Moments:** Create visual prompts that invite customers to engage - turning the store environment from a backdrop into a participant.
- **Fitting Room Visual Enhancement:** For fashion retail, use Deckoviz adjacent to fitting rooms to display complementary styling suggestions or ambient visuals.
- **Customer Memento Creation:** At the moment of purchase, create a personalised artwork for the customer to commemorate their purchase.
- **Waiting Area Engagement:** In spaces where customers wait, use Deckoviz to make the wait feel like a continuation of the experience.

### 3.3 - WINDOW DISPLAYS & EXTERNAL PRESENCE
- **Dynamic Window Display:** Replace static window displays with a living, evolving visual experience visible from outside.
- **Time-of-Day Window Storytelling:** Morning commuters, lunchtime browsers, and evening shoppers have different mindsets - adapt automatically.
- **Campaign Launch Window Takeover:** Take over your window display instantly with new creative - no waiting for installation, no overnight setup.
- **Seasonal Window Transformation:** Move between seasons with your window display in a moment, keeping your exterior presence feeling current and considered year-round.

### 3.4 - MARKETING & CAMPAIGNS
- **Campaign Visual Deployment:** Roll out a new campaign across all your locations simultaneously in seconds - every store updated, on brand, on message, at the exact moment the campaign goes live.
- **Sale and Promotion Display:** Deploy sale visuals with the same care and aesthetic quality as your full-price creative.
- **Lookbook and Editorial Display:** Bring your seasonal lookbooks to life in-store - the same editorial quality and visual world you create online.
- **Collaboration and Partnership Feature:** Give your brand collaborations the in-store visual presence they deserve.
- **Flash Sale and Time-Limited Promotion:** Create urgency around short-term offers with visually compelling displays.
- **Cross-Store Campaign Consistency:** For multi-location retailers, ensure every store is displaying the same creative at the same time.

### 3.5 - BRAND IDENTITY & STORYTELLING
- **Brand Founding Story:** Tell the story of how and why your brand was started - through beautiful visual content that connects customers to the human origin of what they are buying from.
- **Brand Values and Philosophy Display:** Make your brand's values tangible and visible - sustainability, craftsmanship, inclusivity, innovation.
- **Designer or Founder Feature:** For brands with a named creative vision, use Deckoviz to introduce that person.
- **Craft and Production Storytelling:** Show how your products are made - the hands that cut the fabric, the process that formed the material.
- **Supplier and Sourcing Narrative:** For brands that source ethically, sustainably, or with intention, use Deckoviz to celebrate those supply chain decisions.
- **Community and Culture Display:** For brands with a strong community or subculture around them, use Deckoviz to reflect and celebrate that community in-store.

### 3.6 - SEASONAL & EVENTS
- **Holiday Season Transformation:** Transform your store's entire visual identity for Christmas, Diwali, Eid, or any major cultural moment.
- **Product Launch Event Visual Environment:** Give a product launch the in-store visual treatment it deserves.
- **Pop-Up and Temporary Store Experience:** For pop-ups or temporary locations, use Deckoviz to instantly create a complete brand environment.
- **In-Store Event Hosting:** For talks, workshops, styling events, or community gatherings held in-store, use Deckoviz to create a dedicated visual environment.
- **End-of-Season Campaign:** Mark the end of a season with visual elegance - a curated display that honours the season passing while building anticipation for what is coming next.

## PART FOUR: HOTELS

### INTRODUCTION TO HOTELS
A hotel is not a room. It is a feeling. The feeling that begins the moment a guest steps through the door, that follows them through the lobby, the corridor, the restaurant, the spa, and settles with them in the room. The hotels that achieve genuine loyalty are the ones that make that feeling consistent, considered, and personal - and those are not the most expensive hotels. They are the most intentional ones.

Deckoviz gives hotels a tool to express intention at scale. Every surface that has ever held a generic print or a blank space can now do deliberate, beautiful, responsive work. The lobby can feel like a destination. The dining room can feel like a story. The spa can feel like a genuinely healing environment. And every one of these can be updated, adapted, and personalised in seconds - without housekeeping, without maintenance, without designers, and without cost.

The use cases below cover the full arc of the guest experience across every area of a hotel.

### 4.1 - LOBBY & ARRIVAL EXPERIENCE
- **Arrival Ambience Setting:** The lobby is the hotel's opening line - the moment that sets the guest's entire emotional tone for the stay.
- **Time-of-Day Lobby Mood:** Early morning arrivals, afternoon check-ins, and late-night returns all feel different. Deckoviz adapts the lobby's visual character to match the hour.
- **Guest Welcome Display:** Display a personalised welcome for arriving guests - individual names for VIPs, group names for delegations or event bookings.
- **Destination Storytelling:** Help guests connect to the place they have chosen to visit - the city, the neighbourhood, the culture, the history.
- **Hotel Brand Story Introduction:** For guests staying for the first time, use the lobby to tell the story of the hotel.
- **Lobby Art Installation:** Replace static lobby art with a dynamic, evolving visual display that changes over the course of a day, a week, or a season.
- **Concierge Information Display:** Display guest information - today's events, dining hours, weather, transport options.

### 4.2 - GUEST ROOMS & SUITES
- **In-Room Arrival Ambience:** Set a specific visual ambience in the room for guest arrival - a warm welcome scene, a beautifully curated visual, a personalised greeting.
- **Guest Preference Personalisation:** For returning guests with known preferences, use Deckoviz to display visual content aligned with their taste.
- **Celebration Suite Experience:** For guests celebrating a honeymoon, anniversary, or milestone birthday, transform the room's visual environment to mark the occasion.
- **Morning Wake Ambience:** Offer a gentle, light-synced morning sequence that eases guests into the day - natural light-inspired visuals, calm movement.
- **Evening Wind-Down Environment:** As guests return from their day, the room can shift to a warmer, slower, more restful visual atmosphere.
- **In-Room Dining Enhancement:** When a guest orders room service, have the room's visual environment shift to an ambient dining mode.
- **Work Mode for Business Travellers:** For business guests, offer a clean, minimal, structured visual environment that supports focus.

### 4.3 - DINING & F&B
- **Restaurant Ambience by Meal Period:** Breakfast, lunch, afternoon tea, dinner, and late-night bar all warrant different visual atmospheres - and Deckoviz transitions between them automatically.
- **Menu Display and Storytelling:** Present your restaurant's menu through beautifully designed visual displays that reflect the aesthetic quality of the food.
- **Daily Specials and Chef's Table Feature:** Highlight the chef's specials and signature dishes through dedicated visual moments that make the featured items irresistible.
- **Dish Photography and Food Storytelling:** Display stunning food photography and dish narratives that build appetite, communicate quality, and prompt guests.
- **Bar and Cocktail Feature:** Showcase your bar programme, your signature cocktails, and your mixology credentials through visual content.
- **Wine and Beverage Programme Display:** For hotels with a serious wine programme, use Deckoviz to present your cellar, your sommeliers, your pairings, and your story.
- **Private Dining Room Personalisation:** Transform private dining room visuals entirely for each booking - the occasion, the guest's preferences, the event type.

### 4.4 - SPA & WELLNESS
- **Spa Arrival Transition:** The moment a guest enters the spa should feel like a physical transition into a different state - calmer, slower, softer. Deckoviz creates the visual dimension.
- **Treatment Zone Ambience:** Different treatment areas benefit from different visual environments - deep tissue work calls for something different than a facial.
- **Meditation and Breathwork Visualisation:** Offer guided breathing and meditation sessions anchored by beautiful, breath-synced visual experiences.
- **Nature-Inspired Immersive Environments:** Create full immersive visual environments inspired by forests, oceans, mountains, or deserts.
- **Wellness Philosophy Display:** Communicate your spa's treatment philosophy, its approach to wellness, its ingredient sourcing, and its therapeutic lineage.
- **Post-Treatment Relaxation Space:** In recovery areas after treatments, use Deckoviz to maintain the therapeutic atmosphere.
- **Fitness and Training Area Display:** In gyms or fitness facilities, use Deckoviz to create an energising visual environment.

### 4.5 - EVENTS & CONFERENCING
- **Event Space Branding:** For corporate events, conferences, and private bookings, transform the event space's visual identity to match the client's brand.
- **Conference Session Visual Backdrop:** Provide a beautiful, professional visual backdrop for presentations, panel discussions, and keynote addresses.
- **Wedding and Celebration Environment:** For weddings and celebrations, create a completely custom visual environment - the couple's colours, their aesthetic, a visual narrative.
- **Gala and Black-Tie Event Ambience:** For formal evening events, use Deckoviz to create an ambient visual environment that adds drama, elegance, and atmosphere.
- **Exhibition and Showcase Display:** For hotels hosting exhibitions, trade events, or brand showcases, provide exhibitors with a premium visual display platform.
- **Event Schedule and Wayfinding:** For multi-day conferences or events, display schedules, room assignments, speaker information, and directional guidance.

### 4.6 - LOYALTY, UPSELL & COMMERCIAL
